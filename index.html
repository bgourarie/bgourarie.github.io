Watch face for Pebble Time

Custom watch face made for the Pebble time. Shows the time/date in a hideously fantastic Chalkboard font Two white progress bars above/below the time show Battery Life (above) and progress toward daily goal of 12,500 steps (below). Weather icon, Temperature, and actual step count (measured in thousands to one decimal place) go along the bottom.

Solutions for Project Euler

Project Euler is a collection of math problems which require creative, programmatic answers. I've slowly worked through the first twenty, only 380 left to go. I often use these to try out new programming languages or reacquaint myself with one I haven't used recently.

Pebble Time Weather app

Very simple app made for the Pebble Time. Pebble offers a fantastic cloud based IDE for making apps. I followed one of their tutorials to create a simple app that fetches the hourly weather forecast for the next 30 hours and displays it in a simple list.
